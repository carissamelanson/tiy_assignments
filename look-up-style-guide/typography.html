Typography

Rooney Sans Web is only used in the logo. Rooney Sans Web is a round font with soft terminals that gives the logo a slightly gentle and accessible feel.

The Brandon Grotesque family is used in 2 weights through out the site. The typeface has a functional look with a warm touch. The geometric style of the typeface reflects the star pattern that is used in the hero image. The 700 weight is well suited for headlines, while the regular weight works well in body text.
