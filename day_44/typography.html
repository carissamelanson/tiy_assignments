Typography

I explored typefaces to find one that I thought represented the values of Blue Dahlia Bistro and reminiscent of European bistros. I used Aw Conqueror Carved by Typofonderie for the headers and AW Conqueror Carved One for sub headers. I paired the AW Conqueror typefaces with Adobe Caslon Pro for the body copy. The typefaces were a nice marriage of classic meets modern.
